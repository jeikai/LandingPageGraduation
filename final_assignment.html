Caps in the Air, Hearts Full of Hope: Reflections on University Graduation: Signatures of Graduation Ceremonies

Graduation day is more than a ceremony - it's a whirlwind of emotions and memories. As caps soar into the sky, graduates' hearts swell with pride, joy, and anticipation for the future. This moment marks the end of an era and the dawn of new adventures in the labour market.

7/15/2024, 12:00:00 PM

HNUE
One of Vietnam's oldest universities, Hanoi National University of Education, has a unique graduation tradition where female graduates wear white Ao Dai.

“This traditional image is not only a hallmark of the university's graduation but also embodies the Vietnamese tradition of "respecting teachers and valuing education." - Duong Do Phuong Vy - QH2019 HNUE

ULIS

“A bird would perch in a safe place” - Hoang Quynh Anh - QH2020 FELTE ULIS

Tranquill and diverse are the perfect words to describe ULIS’s graduation with dearing pigeons and a unique backdrop for each graduate.

University of Economics and Business (UEB)

UEB

UEB - the “royal” university has always been known for holding distinctive graduation ceremonies with the two signatures of the Parade led by the Rector’s Mace and the formal Bachelor gown.

Graduation Parade

Having a parade of the rector and the ceremonial team has always been UEB’s tradition during the graduation ceremony. The parade is led by the Mace, which symbolizes the Rector’s leadership, protecting the university and its students against negativity and serving as a reminder to continue their pursuits.

Bachelor gown

The colours of the graduation attire collectively represent a blend of clarity (teal robe), passion and determination (red trim), excellence and achievement (gold). They are carefully chosen to embody the qualities that the graduates have developed during their time at the university to carry into their future.

AOF

Academy of Finance (AOF)

The university with the most "lavish" graduation ceremony in 2024 is likely to be AOF. From investing in photo banners and backdrops around the campus to giving various commemorative gifts to new graduates, the celebration is truly caring.
