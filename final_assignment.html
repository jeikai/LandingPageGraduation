HNUE
One of Vietnam's oldest universities, Hanoi National University of Education, has a unique graduation tradition where female graduates wear white Ao Dai. This attire symbolises purity, knowledge, and the enduring beauty of Vietnamese female teachers, highlighting the graduates' grace and achievements.

ULIS

A bird would perch in a safe place

Tranquill is the perfect word to describe ULIS’s graduation with dearing pigeons and a wonderful lotus pond on campus.

Lotus pond - Inner peace for students

The favourite check-in destination of Ulisers is the lotus pond. Now that I have graduated, I will miss this place so much.” - Hoang Quynh Anh, a Bachelor graduate of QH2020.

University of Economics and Business (UEB)

Mace - the protection

The traditional Mace of UEB symbolizes the Rector’s leadership, protection for the university and its students against negativity, and serves as a reminder to continue their academic and career pursuits.

Bachelor gown

The colours of the graduation attire collectively represent a blend of clarity (teal robe), passion and determination (red trim), excellence and achievement (gold). They are carefully chosen to embody the qualities that the graduates have developed during their time at the university to carry into their future.

AOF

Academy of Finance (AOF)

The university with the most "lavish" graduation ceremony in 2024 is likely to be AOF. From investing in photo banners and backdrops around the campus to giving various commemorative gifts to new graduates, the celebration is truly caring.
