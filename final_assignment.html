One of Vietnam's oldest universities, Hanoi National University of Education, has a unique graduation tradition where female graduates wear white Ao Dai. This attire symbolises purity, knowledge, and the enduring beauty of Vietnamese female teachers, highlighting the graduates' grace and achievements.
